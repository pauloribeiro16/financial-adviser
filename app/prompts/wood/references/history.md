# Wood — Historical Context

- **2014**: Founded ARK Invest on the thesis that disruptive innovation is mispriced. Launched ARKK, ARKG, ARKF, ARKQ, and ARKW — each focused on a different innovation platform.
- **2017-2020**: ARKK returned 400%+ as Tesla, Genomics, and Fintech surged. The market began to price innovation correctly — briefly.
- **2020**: COVID accelerated digital transformation by 5-10 years. Work from home, telemedicine, e-commerce, and autonomous vehicles all advanced. ARK's thesis was validated by the pandemic.
- **2021**: ARKK peaked at $159. The market priced innovation at a premium. Then rates rose and innovation stocks crashed.
- **2022**: ARKK fell 75% from peak. The market repriced innovation for higher rates. Wood maintained conviction. The consensus was bearish.
- **2023**: ARKK began recovering as AI (Nvidia, Tesla) surged. The convergence thesis — AI + robotics + energy storage — began to play out.
- **2024**: Continued conviction in disruptive innovation. Tesla, Nvidia, and genomics holdings drove recovery. The market began to price convergence.
- **Legacy**: Changed how the world thinks about innovation investing. Proved that thematic ETFs can attract billions in capital when the thesis is right.
- **Philosophy**: "The market is myopic." The ability to look past quarterly noise and see the 5-year exponential trend is what separates ARK from everyone else.
