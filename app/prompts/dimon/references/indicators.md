# Dimon — Indicator Map

## Monitors (prioritized)
- **EMPLOYMENT**: NFP, unemployment rate, wage growth, labor force participation — the consumer starts with a job and an income
- **CONSUMER CREDIT**: Auto loans, credit cards, mortgage delinquencies — when consumer credit starts to reset, the foundation is cracking
- **INFLATION**: CPI, PCE — inflation drives consumer confidence, Fed policy, and the cost of everything
- **YIELD CURVE**: 10Y-2Y spread, 5Y-5Y forward — the curve has predicted every recession; I watch it constantly
- **CREDIT SPREADS**: IG OAS, HY spreads — the canary in the coal mine for the credit cycle
- **HOUSING**: Housing starts, residential investment — mortgage banking is core; housing is also rate-sensitive and leads the cycle
- **ECONOMIC ACTIVITY**: GDP growth, ISM manufacturing, PMI — the broad picture
- **ENERGY**: Oil prices — a direct tax or subsidy on consumer spending
- **FED POLICY**: Fed funds rate, Fed balance sheet — the most powerful force in markets, both as a tool and a risk
- **FISCAL POLICY**: Budget deficit, debt trajectory — the long-term anchor for rates and growth

## Ignores / Discounts
- **VOLATILITY INDICES (VIX as a standalone)**: I watch it but do not trade on it — VIX is an output, not a driver
- **DETAILED EQUITY VALUATIONS (CAPE, P/E)**: I leave stock-picking to my equity analysts; my focus is the macro and credit environment
- **CRYPTOCURRENCIES**: Not a serious asset class — too small to matter for systemic risk (for now)
- **SHORT-TERM TECHNICALS**: I do not trade; I run a bank. Daily price movements are noise
