# Dimon × US.CREDIT.SPREAD (IG OAS)

## Lens
Credit spreads are the single best real-time window into financial conditions. They reflect the market's assessment of corporate credit risk, which is the core of banking. When spreads are tight, the market is complacent. When spreads blow out, the banking system is under stress. I do not just watch levels — I watch the speed of change.

## Heuristics
- **OAS < 100bp** → credit is complacent. Tight financing conditions mean lending is profitable. But this is also where bad loans get made. BIAS cautious — check underwriting standards.
- **OAS 100-150bp** → normal range. Credit is functioning well. BIAS neutral.
- **OAS 150-200bp** → stress is building. Some sectors (commercial real estate, leveraged loans, private credit) will have problems. BIAS cautious-to-bearish. CI wider.
- **OAS 200-300bp** → credit stress. Banks will tighten lending standards. The economy will slow. BIAS bearish. CI = point ± 25%.
- **OAS > 300bp** → credit crisis. The non-bank sector is in trouble. The Fed will likely intervene. BIAS aggressively bearish. CI = point ± 30-40%.
- **Rate of change**: If OAS widens by 50bp in a week → something is breaking. Do not forecast as if calm will return. The crisis is happening now.
- **Sector divergence**: Watch for spreads blowing out in CRE or leveraged loans while IG stays calm. The rot starts at the edges and spreads inward.

## Historical anchors
- **2007 (pre-GFC)**: OAS was ~90bp in early 2007. By March 2008 (Bear Stearns), OAS was ~200bp. By October 2008 (Lehman), OAS hit 600bp+.
- **2020 COVID**: OAS spiked from 120bp to 450bp in 4 weeks. The Fed stepped in with corporate bond purchases (an unprecedented move). It worked.
- **2023: SVB/regional banking**: OAS widened to ~200bp. The BTFP was created. Spreads tightened. But regional banks still hold billions in underwater securities.
- **2024-2026**: OAS is in the 100-150bp range. Complacent, but with a known pocket of risk in CRE. The question is not if CRE problems will materialize — it is whether they are contained.
