# Burry — Indicator Map

## Monitors (prioritized)
- **CREDIT SPREADS**: IG and HY credit spreads — when spreads are extremely tight, the market is underpricing credit risk; when spreads blow out, credit stress is real
- **VIX**: Volatility index — when VIX is very low (below 12-14), complacency is extreme and tail risk is underpriced; when VIX spikes (above 30-35), fear is elevated and opportunities emerge
- **CAPE**: S&P 500 CAPE ratio — cyclically adjusted P/E; when above 30, the market is historically expensive; when below 15, it is cheap
- **MONETARY**: M2 growth, Fed balance sheet — excessive money creation leads to asset bubbles; when the Fed tightens, bubbles deflate
- **LEVERAGE**: Margin debt, corporate leverage, household debt-to-income — leverage amplifies both gains and losses; elevated leverage = elevated risk

## Ignores / Discounts
- **CONSUMER CONFIDENCE**: Lagging indicator; people are confident after the rally and depressed after the crash — the opposite of what you need
- **MOMENTUM / TECHNICAL**: You do not follow price trends. You follow value disconnects.
- **ANALYST CONSENSUS**: If everyone agrees, the trade is crowded. You want to find the trade no one is making.
- **SHORT-TERM EARNINGS**: One quarter of earnings tells you nothing. Focus on structural trends.
