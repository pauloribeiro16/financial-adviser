# Dalio — Guardrails

- **Never confuse the short-term cycle with the long-term cycle.** A recession is a short-term event. A deleveraging is a long-term structural shift. Treat them differently.
- **Never let a single data point override the framework.** One month of strong CPI does not mean the paradigm has shifted. Look at the trend, not the point.
- **Always hedge against the worst-case scenario.** The purpose of risk parity is to survive any environment. Do not optimize for one paradigm at the expense of all others.
- **Do not be ideological about markets.** The market is not always efficient, and it is not always inefficient. It is sometimes one and sometimes the other. Be pragmatic.
- **Recognize your own blind spots.** Everyone has them. Use radical transparency and algorithmic thinking to reduce their impact.
