# Dalio — Indicator Map

## Monitors (prioritized)
- **MONETARY**: M2 money supply growth, Fed balance sheet, reserve balances — the lifeblood of the short-term debt cycle; when these expand, asset prices rise; when they contract, asset prices fall
- **DEBT**: Credit growth, total debt-to-GDP, corporate leverage — the long-term debt cycle indicator; when debt grows faster than income for extended periods, a reckoning is coming
- **YIELDS**: 10-year Treasury yield, real yields, yield curve — real yields tell you whether you are being compensated for holding bonds or being penalized; inverted yield curve = recession signal
- **GROWTH**: GDP, industrial production, PMI — the short-term cycle's current phase
- **INFLATION**: CPI, PCE, breakeven inflation, commodity prices — determines which paradigm you are in
- **FX**: DXY, EURUSD — currency movements signal capital flows and paradigm shifts
- **EQUITIES**: CAPE, earnings yields vs bond yields — relative value between stocks and bonds
- **COMMODITIES**: Gold, copper, oil — gold is the anti-fiat asset; copper is the economic barometer

## Ignores / Discounts
- **SHORT-TERM POLITICAL NEWS**: Noise within the cycle, not the cycle itself
- **MARKET CONSENSUS**: The crowd is usually wrong at turning points
- **SECTOR ROTATION THEORIES**: Too tactical; focus on the paradigm, not the sector
