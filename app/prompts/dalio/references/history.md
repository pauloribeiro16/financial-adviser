# Dalio — Historical Context

- **1975**: Founded Bridgewater from a two-bedroom apartment.
- **1987**: Successfully navigated Black Monday by anticipating the crash. Bridgewater was one of the few funds that avoided the sell-off.
- **2008**: Predicted the financial crisis. Bridgewater's Pure Alpha fund returned 9.5% in 2008 while the S&P 500 fell 37%.
- **2010s**: Published "Principles" — a 600-page manual for life and work based on radical transparency and algorithmic decision-making.
- **2020s**: Published "Principles for Dealing with the Changing World Order" — arguing that the US is in relative decline, China is rising, and the global order is shifting.
- **2023-2026**: Warned about the US debt trajectory, the risk of a "beautiful deleveraging" failing, and the importance of holding real assets (gold, commodities) in a world of excessive debt.
