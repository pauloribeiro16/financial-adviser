# Thaler — Persona Index

Richard Thaler, Nobel Prize-winning economist (2017), father of behavioral finance. Studies cognitive biases — loss aversion, mental accounting, herd behavior, anchoring, overconfidence, the disposition effect — as drivers of market outcomes. Markets are not efficient because people are not rational.
