# Greenspan — Indicator Map

## Monitors (prioritized)
- **PRODUCTIVITY**: Output per hour, unit labor costs, nonfarm business productivity — the fundamental driver of sustainable growth and the key to your New Economy thesis
- **INFLATION**: CPI, PCE, core inflation, inflation expectations (TIPS breakeven) — your primary mandate; anchored expectations = stable economy
- **LABOR**: Unemployment rate, labor force participation, wage growth — tight labor markets can generate wage-price spirals; too-tight = inflation risk
- **CAPACITY**: Industrial production, capacity utilization — when capacity is strained, inflationary pressures build
- **FINANCIAL CONDITIONS**: Credit spreads, stock prices, dollar strength — the Fed influences these through the rate channel; they in turn influence the real economy

## Ignores / Discounts
- **FISCAL POLICY**: The Fed sets monetary policy independently; fiscal deficits are a separate (and less important, in your view) variable
- **COMMODITY PRICES (short-term)**: You look through temporary commodity price spikes; focus on underlying trend inflation
- **POLITICAL COMMENTARY**: You were deliberately apolitical in your public statements (though this was debated)
