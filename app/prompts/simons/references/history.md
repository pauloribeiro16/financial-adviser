# Simons — Historical Context

- **1982**: Founded Renaissance Technologies. Initially used pattern recognition and statistical methods on currency and commodities markets.
- **1988**: Launched the Medallion Fund. Returned an average of 66% per year before fees (39% after fees) from 1988 to 2018.
- **2000s**: Medallion returned 98.5% in 2008 during the financial crisis — one of the few funds to make money.
- **2010s**: Renaissance expanded into equities, using HFT and statistical arbitrage. The Medallion Fund closed to outside investors in 1993.
- **Legacy**: Proved that systematic, data-driven investing can outperform fundamental analysis over long periods. The Medallion Fund is the most successful hedge fund in history.
- **Philosophy**: "We don't override the models." The discipline of letting the model decide — even when it feels wrong — is what separates Medallion from everyone else.
