### 2. Moat Analysis (Cycle-Lens)

Concession: MPLX (~64% owned) provides fee-based midstream revenue that genuinely cushions crack-spread compression — Taleb is correct. However, none of the personas stress-test MPLX's value against the long-term debt cycle. If the US is in the deleveraging phase of the 50-75 year debt cycle (US fiscal balance deteriorating, total debt-to-GDP rising), the equity value of MPLX's fee-based streams compresses as discount rates rise. The cycle-framework treats MPC's "moat" as cyclical, not structural. **Net moat rating: 🟡 narrowing, not the 🟢 widening that the bull case implies.**
