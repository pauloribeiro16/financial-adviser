### 3. Growth Engines (Cycle-Lens)

The 10-K narrative cites 3.0M bpd refining capacity and 730 mmgy Martinez Renewables JV as growth drivers. But renewable diesel is a regulated-margin business exposed to RIN/LCFS compliance costs (10-K Business & Market Risk section). In a paradigm where inflation is persistent (UST10Y 4.06% implies restrictive real yields are sticky), RIN credit values compress and renewable diesel margins shrink. None of the growth engines hold up in a higher-for-longer paradigm. **Growth Engines rating shifts from 🟢/🟡 (personas) to 🟡/🔴 through cycle lens.**
